White Rice with Grill Chicken Breast

Description

After your breakfast and half of the day done, you need to eat some lunch because we cannot be overwork and not having lunch >:V. Anyways, The best meal (in my honest and broke opinion) is White Chicken with Grill Rice. Certainly a gift from the gods themselves, Amaterasu would be proud.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Adobo or any other seasoning of your liking
  • Oil
  • A cast iron or frying pan

Steps

  1. Put a cup of water to boil, Add salt at your liking now that is at room temperature, and at a tablespoon or two of Oil. This is to avoid the rice to get stuck.
  2. When the Water is boiling, 100 Celsius or 212 Fahrenheit in freedom units, add the Rice, and let it boil
  3. While the water boils, take your chicken and beat it bit a hammer(a cooking hammer is ok)
  4. Add some salt, pepper, and Seasoning to your chicken
  5. Put some oil on the pan and put your chicken there.
  6. Flip it regularly for 15-20 minutes and take it out of the pan
  7. Wait 10 minutes more and your rice is ready.
