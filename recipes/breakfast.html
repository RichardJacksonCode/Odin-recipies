Egg, Ham, and Cheese Sandwich

Description

When you wake up iin the morning the first and most important thing that you need to do is to get some energy to start your day, and there isn't any other way to get that energy than to eat a good and healthy breakfast. After all, it is the most important food in the day

Ingredients

  • Any type of Bread Works
  • Ham
  • 2 Eggs
  • Slices of Cheese
  • Butter
  • Span

Steps

  1. Spray span on a frying pan and let it heat, and when it does, put your some slices of ham on it. As many slices as you want
  2. At the same time that the ham is cooking, grab your two eggs and break them on a small container.
  3. Add some salt, pepper, oregano, and adobo and scramble them
  4. When you do all of this your ham must be hot enough so, take it out of your pan, add more span, and put the eggs on the pan.
  5. As the eggs cooked and take some form, put the cheese on top of the eggs, then the ham, more cheese and fold the eggs like a book
  6. Put some butter on the bread, and put the egg, ham, and cheese inside of it
  7. at this point you can eat the sandwich but if you want to tost it, you can put it on the pat, and flip it when you like it.

An easy recipe to eat a good breakfast and start your day right.
