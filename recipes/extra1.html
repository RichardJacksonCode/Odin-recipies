White Rice with Fry Chicken

Description

After spending half day working or studying you are now ready to eat some lunch, in this case, we are going to eat white rice and fry chicken.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Salt, Pepper, Seasoning
  • Oil
  • Any piece of chicken
  • White Flour
  • Eggs
  • Chicken Bread Dust

Steps

  1. Put a cup of rice on a pot with salt and oil
  2. Wait until it build and put the rice
  3. Take a fork and stab the hell out of the chicken
  4. Put the White flour on a bowl, add salt, pepper and seasoning, add The Chicken Bread Dust and mix everything.
  5. Submerge the chicken on eggs
  6. Later submerge in white flour with the seasoning and the Bread Dust
  7. Drop the Chicken on the hot oil(don't burn yourself)
  8. Serve the chicken and the rice and eat it
