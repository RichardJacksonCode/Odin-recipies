Beef Tacos

Description

Welcome to the extra section, a special website just for you that will teach you something interesting, in this case I will teach you how to make Beef tacos. Buckle up and enjoy the ride

Ingredients

  • Tacos's Soul and Skin
  • Ground Beef
  • Prego Sauce(Marinara is good)
  • Salt, pepper, and Salt Seasoning
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put your ground beef on a pot and add the Prego Sauce, stir it
  2. As the meat get heat, use a cup of water and add salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder and add it to your meat, stir it again
  3. After 10 minutes of cooking add your taco seasoning
  4. Let it cook for like 20 minutes and move it regularly, before taking it out of the pot, taste it and add seasoning if you want to
  5. Put the taco skin on a Pan and us the soul to sacrifice it to Satan so you can have a delicious meal
  6. put cheese, put the meat, and put the cheese again
  7. DONE
